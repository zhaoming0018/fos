Mathematics and Applied Mathematics

Program Objectives:

　　

Students with a B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics will have basic knowledge of mathematics, physics, mathematical application,engineering and computer, have good knowledge structure and strong innovation ability, and engage in mathematical research, application, development and promotion. They often seek employment in education, industry, utilizing their skills to solve engineering and computer related problems.

Courses:

　 　

Mathematical Analysis, Linear Algebra, Abstract Algebra, Analytic Geometry, Differential Geometry, Functions of Complex Variables, Functions of Real Variables, Ordinary Differential Equations, Functional Analysis, Topology of Set of Points, Probability and Statistics, Elementary Number Theory, Computational Methods, Mathematical Modeling, College Physics, Basic of Computer and Programming.

Mathematics and Applied Mathematics （Normal）

Program Objectives:

　　

Students with a B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics（Normal） will have following abilities of a solid mathematical basis and wider range of knowledge, high quality, scientific creativity. They will have a substantial interest in teaching mathematics at the secondary and high schools' levels.

Courses:

　

　Mathematical Analysis, Linear Algebra, Abstract Algebra, Analytic Geometry, Functions of Complex Variables, Functions of Real Variables, Ordinary Differential Equations, Functional Analysis, Discrete Mathematics, Probability and Statistics, Education, Psychology, Mathematics Education, Modern Teaching Techniques, Research on Elementary Mathematics.

Information and Computational Science

Program Objectives:

　　

Students with a B.S. degree in Information and Computational Science will have ability to have fundamental theory, basic knowledge and skills of information theory, scientific computing , software development and design, and information processing and coding, have the primary training of basic and scientific development researches, have good scientific and creative spirits and practical ability, and fit in with the needs of Chinese economy and society development.

Courses:

　　

Mathematical Analysis, Linear Algebra, Analytic Geometry, Probability and Statistics,Mathematical Modeling, College Physics, Basic of Computer and Programming, Information Processing, Information Coding and Information Security, Computational Intelligence, Optimization Theory, Computer Graphics, Algorithms and Data Structures, Software Systems, Operating System.

Mathematics and Applied Mathematics（Economics, International Program）

Program Objectives:

　　

Students with a B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics （Economics, International Program） will have basic knowledge of mathematics, economics and mathematical application, have good knowledge structure and strong innovation ability, and engage in financial mathematical research and application. Normal physics

Program Objectives:

The aim is to make the students to become excellent talented persons who can master the basic theories, knowledge and experiment skills of physics. They will be professional talented persons who can teach students and make research in the colleges and universities or middle schools.

Courses:

　　

The main courses include the advanced mathematics, the mechanics, the calorifics, the electromagnetics, the optics, the modern physics, the theoretical mechanics, the electrodynamics, the statistical physics, the quantum mechanics, the mathematical methods of physics, the electronic circuit, the experiments in general physics, the physics teaching theory, the pedagogics, the psychology, the computer and application, the modern educational technology, and the foundation of humanity social sciences. The educational period is four years.

Applied physical technology

Program Objectives:

The aim is to make the students to become excellent talented persons who have the good foundations of mathematics, the applied foundations of computers, and prominent physics technique and skills. They can master the basic theories and methods of physics well. They are professional talented persons who can teach students and make research in some fields including physics, photoelectron technology, or physical detection.

Courses:

　　

The main courses include the general physics, the theoretical physics, the Mathematical Methods of Physics, the mathematical electronic technique, the analog electronic technique, the information optics, the principles of laser, the semiconductor physics and devices, the optoelectronics, the fundamentals of sensors and technology, and the fundamentals of mono-chip computers & applications. The educational period is four years.
